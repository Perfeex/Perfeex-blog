Why is a Modern Simple Saree a Must-Have?

Simple sarees, modern versions of the age-old attire, go down quite well with the traditional and the modern styles. These sarees are minimalist versions of the original, easy to wear at work or to some special event. For the growing demand for lightweight and easy-to-drape fabrics with subtle designs, these sarees do indeed give an understated elegance with a dash of modernity.

Unlike the others, which are heavy and challenging in their weavings and embroidery, modern simple sarees have clean cut lines with minimal use of embroidery and sleek patterns, making them versatile and convenient for you to wear to work, casual outings, or even formal events. Let's dive into why these sarees are taking the fashion world by storm.

The Charm of the Modern Simple Sarees:

  1. Ease of Draping: The modern saree is made in georgette, chiffon, and linen fabrics, which are extremely light and easy to drape as compared to their heavy counterparts such as silk or brocade.
  2. Minimalistic Designs: Most of the times, they are low-key designs. These sarees are fabulous when one wants a simple elegance. A plain color with subtle border work or prints says it all.
  3. Versatile Styling: Highly versatile, modern sarees can be styled in multiple ways. The wearer can pair them with a trendy blouse, crop top, or even with a structured jacket to create an instant chic look.
  4. Fit for All Occasions: Modern simple sarees fit any kind of wardrobe, from casual daywear to formal evening events. So one needs to style them appropriately according to the occasion.

How to Get the Best Modern Saree Style

Wearing a modern saree would be where classic elegance blends with the present trends. Some tips on how to get the perfect saree modern look would be as follows:

1. Pair the Saree with Modern Blouses

Modernizing your saree is one of the easiest and simplest things because with the top you wear, it can easily get a trendy look. A properly-fitted crop top, sleeveless, or even an off-shoulder could instantly give your saree the contemporary edge that everyone will be dressing for today. Popular with young ladies wearing a saree but with a modern twist.

2. Use Less Accessories

Gone are the days when heavy, chunky jewelry was the norm. For keeping the look modern and elegant, minimalistic accessories should be worn. Statement necklaces, sleek bangles, and geometric earrings can complete the look without overpowering the simplicity of the saree.

3. Try On Drape Styles

Traditional draping styles could always go eternal, but try experimenting with new-age draping techniques by adding some modern glamour to it. Try the pant-style drape, dhoti drape, or even cinch the saree around your waist with a belt for a more structured, modern look.

4. Choose Big, Bold Solid Colors

Solid colors in rich shades such as deep blue, emerald green, and classic black will make you look elegant yet at the same time, modern. These generally are not much of a styling effort and can be dressed up or down for any occasion.

5. Play with Footwear

You can also add footwear to elevate the saree look. While juttis or more so, the traditional type of sandals are popular, you can change things a little by pairing your modern saree with stilettos or ankle boots or even sneakers for a bold but casual look.

Frequently Asked Questions About Modern Sarees

Q1: What makes a saree modern?

A: This usually includes simple designs, lightweight fabrics, and contemporary styling. A modern saree is very easy to wear. It's both comfortable for daily use and suitable for formal events.

Q2: Can I wear this at the wedding?

A: Absolutely. Contemporary sarees, especially silk or chiffon, do make for great wear if accessorized and styled rightly. They do look totally respectable on the wedding day with proper statement jewelry and a great blouse.

Q3: How do I style a saree with crop top?

A: Tandem the saree with crop top: crop top and saree is the latest way of dressing fashionably. Crop tops can be chosen as per the color or pattern according to which will go well with the color of saree or can be contrasted. You may go for crop tops of various lengths of sleeves and necklines to create a characteristic look.

Q4: Which material is suitable for saris nowadays?

A: Georgette, chiffon, cotton, and linen types are required in making modern sarees as they are lightweight, easy to drape, and available in a sleek silhouette.

Q5: Where can I find branded modern sarees online?

A: Branded modern sarees are easily found on popular websites such as Amazon, Flipkart, Myntra, or specialty stores like FabIndia and Biba.
