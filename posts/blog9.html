Take care of Your Vans clothing.

To keep your Vans sweatshirts, hoodies, and sweaters in the best condition possible, here are some basic care tips:

  • Wash: Always wash your Vans clothing in cold water on a gentle cycle in order to prevent shrinkage and damage to the fabric.
  • Drying: Air drying is best, but if using a dryer, low heat is recommended.
  • Ironing: If so, iron your Vans pieces at a low setting and avoid directly touching the logos or prints.
  • Store your hoodies and sweaters in a cool, dry place to prevent fading or stretching.
